Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Accounts are created by your nutrition coach. Contact your coach and they will set up your account with your email address. You'll then be able to log in and start tracking.

How do I scan a barcode?

Go to Meal Plan, tap "+ Add Food" on any meal, switch to the "Scan Barcode" tab, and either use the camera scanner or type the barcode number manually. Nutritional data is pulled from the OpenFoodFacts database.

What if a product isn't found when I scan it?

If a barcode isn't in our database, you'll see an "Add New Product" form. Enter the nutritional information from the product packaging and it will be saved for the whole community to use in future.

How do I track my weight?

On the Dashboard, scroll down to "Weight Tracking". Enter your weight in kg or lbs and tap "Log". You can view your weight history over 7 days, 14 days, 30 days, 90 days, or 1 year using the range buttons.

How do I message my coach?

Tap the "Inbox" tab in the navigation bar to send and receive messages with your assigned coach.

How do I change my password?

Tap your name in the top right corner to open the profile menu, then select "Change Password".

Is the app free?

Yes. No Rule Nutrition is completely free with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at norulesnutrition@gmail.com and we will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Nutritional Data Sources

Nutritional information displayed in the app is sourced from OpenFoodFacts, an open-source, community-contributed food products database. While we strive for accuracy, nutritional values may vary by product batch and region. Always check product packaging for the most accurate information.

Important: No Rule Nutrition is a food tracking tool, not a medical device. The nutritional information and macro tracking features are intended for general wellness and fitness purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making significant changes to your diet, especially if you have medical conditions or specific dietary requirements.
